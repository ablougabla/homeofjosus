RESUME

Featured Project: AEV

The second half of my freshman year consisted of me working in a team of four to build an AEV (advanced energy vehicle). The vehicle was powered by an Arduino microcontroller, which spun dual motors, and producing thrust. I was particularly responsible for supervising microcontroller code along with teammate Eric and for the overall design of the vehicle (drafted in Solidworks). As you can probably tell, the team and I went for something lightweight and minimalistic, but with big propellers, thus big power.

You can view the system analysis, one of many team write-ups, here. This one was prepared by yours truly.
